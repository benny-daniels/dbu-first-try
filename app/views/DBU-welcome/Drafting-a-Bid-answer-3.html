Surrey County Council - Digital Transformation Partner

Criteria: Referenced proof of experience of delivering improved customer outcomes and monetary savings within public sector services including Adults and Childrens social care, Special Educational Needs.

The Bid Submitted by The PSC:

The Royal Surrey County Hospital and Surrey adult care team asked us improve the way frail elderly patients were handled. Patients were staying too long in hospital, leading to both bottlenecks in the hospital and increased patient frailty. Our flow analysis and mapping led us to co-deliver new processes and measurements within and between the two organisations. Together we reduced by 20% the amount of time frail elderly patients spent in the acute setting. This delivered better patient experiece and outcomes and saved the local health and social care economy £9m per year.
