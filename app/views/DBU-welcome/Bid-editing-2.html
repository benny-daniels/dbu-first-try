Bid 2

Criteria: Agile delivery - Demonstrate a track-record of using Agile approaches to successfully deliver projects, preferably in the public sector

Bid: We led the Agile, end-to-end, paper-to-digital transformation of FCDO’s complex system for managing international staff moves. The project was conducted in an entirely Agile manner, upskilling members of the multi-disciplinary team in Agile approaches where needed. We undertook extensive user research, then developed a prototype, which we tested with both end-users and back-office staff, resulting in dozens of iterations. Throughout Discovery, Alpha and Beta, we held Agile ceremonies including standups, show-and-tells and retros, and utilised Agile software such as Slack, Jira and Trello. The resulting service has reduced the time required to move staff by 90% and eliminated errors entirely.
