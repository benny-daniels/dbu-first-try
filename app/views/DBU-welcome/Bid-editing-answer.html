Submitted Bid

Criteria: User centred design - Demonstrate experience in understanding how to meet the needs of a diverse range of users, and a demonstrable commitment to accessibility

Bid: We took a fully user-centred approach to the end-to-end development of FCDO's Emergency Travel Documents service, for citizens with lost/stolen passports needing to get home. The service had to be universally accessible, including to users with disabilities (e.g. dyslexia, visual impairments), limited digital literacy/skills, and hardware/connectivity issues, as well as mobile responsive. We undertook extensive user-testing of the solution, working with FCDO's disability network, the Digital Accessibility Centre, and charities including AgeUK. This resulted in a WCAG-compliant, highly-usable live service which passed Service Standard Assessment. It successfully repatriates c.30k people annually and receives completion and satisfaction scores of >80%.
