Drafting a Bid

For this exercise you are invited to practice and refine your bid writing skills. Below is a brief summary of a project The PSC has bid on, similar to as it would appear on the Digital Marketplace. You will be asked to draft a potential 100 word bid for the project, with special attention given to the highlighted criteria. Please search for key terms within our database of 100 word answers to gather examples used in previous bids to help you draft your response.

Remember to demonstrate how The PSC meets the 'essential' and 'nice-to-have' skills and experience highlighted by the advert. Once you click submit, you will be able to compare your draft bid to the bid The PSC submitted for this work. Each bid shown in this exercise will have recieved a score of 3, meaning it will have exceeded the criteria and may be seen as an exemplar.

Bid 1

Discovery Delivery Partner for the Better Outcomes through Linked Data (BOLD) programme

Work summary: We require the expertise of a highly skilled Discovery Partner with a proven track record of successful delivery to deliver an initial discovery phase to scope, initiate and set the strategy for the Better Outcomes through Linked Data (BOLD) programme, a new project aimed at linking cross-govt. data on vulnerable people to provide more joined up and effective services and build an improved evidence base to support policy creation and operational decision-making. The chosen partner will work with us for 3 months to scope what the programme will deliver and how, ensuring it builds upon what has gone before and meets a range of user needs from across local and national government.

Criteria: Technical skills and experience in data linking, integration and engineering.

Tip: Remember to reflect on a previous case study to support your answer.

Type your bid here. Max 100 words.
