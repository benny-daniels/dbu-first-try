Discovery Delivery Partner for the Better Outcomes through Linked Data (BOLD) programme

Criteria: Technical skills and experience in data linking, integration and engineering.

The Bid Submitted by The PSC:

We supported the OneLondon partnership to enable patient data linking, integration and engineering for record-sharing across London NHS Trusts. This included:

  • Designing and engineering a secure, API-driven 'Health Information Exchange' using multiple open-source technologies (e.g. React, Node, REDIS)
  • Consolidating, normalising, and exchanging data (20+ sources) in an abstraction layer
  • Defining an open data standard and rolling-out the specifics of cleansing changes to hundreds of data-controllers across STPs' providers.

The linking and integration of data enabled patient records to be exchanged across London's STPs, improving journeys and outcomes for our use-cases. Our approach has been promoted by NHSX for projects nationwide.
